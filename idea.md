# 🎮 Discord Engagement Minigame Ideas (2026)

To keep your community active and fun, here is a categorized list of minigames you can implement.

---

## 🛠️ Category 1: Custom Coded Games
*These are games I can help you code directly into your current bot (`Test Bot 1`).*

### 1. The Counting Challenge
*   **How it works:** Members count to the highest number possible. If someone counts out of order, the number resets to 0. 
*   **Why it works:** It creates a massive "don't mess it up" community goal.

### 2. High/Low Betting
*   **How it works:** The bot generates a random number (1-100). Members bet "High" or "Low" on what the next number will be.
*   **Why it works:** Simple, fast-paced, and can be tied to a server economy.

### 3. Word Chain (Shiritori)
*   **How it works:** Each word must start with the last letter of the word before it (e.g., Apple -> Elephant -> Tiger).
*   **Why it works:** Keeps people typing and active in specific channels.

## 🤖 Category 2: Popular Existing Bots
*Deep engagement systems you can add to your server right now.*

### 1. TacoShack (Economy & Strategy)
*   **Concept:** Run a virtual taco restaurant. Purchase upgrades, hire staff, and compete with other "franchises" in your server.
*   **Vibe:** Addictive roleplay/economy.

### 2. PokeTwo (Collection)
*   **Concept:** Pokemon spawn randomly while people chat. The first person to "catch" them owns it.
*   **Vibe:** Extreme engagement; people will chat more just to trigger a spawn.

### 3. Gartic Bot (Visual/Interactive)
*   **Concept:** The bot posts an AI-generated or drawn image piece-by-piece, and members have to guess what it is.
*   **Vibe:** High-energy casual fun.

---

## 🏆 Category 3: Community RPG & Social
*Games that depend more on member interaction than bot math.*

### 1. Mafia / Werewolf
*   **Concept:** A text-based version of the classic deception game. Roles are assigned privately, and the server must discuss and find the killer.

### 2. Epic RPG
*   **Concept:** A text-based dungeon crawler. Members "hunt," "chop," and "fish" to gather resources and fight bosses together.

### 3. Trivia Nights
*   **Concept:** Scheduled events where the bot asks questions across different categories (Movies, Games, History) for special server roles.
